Air Traffic Control

Job Description

  • Taking off and landing instructions to pilot
  • Direct and monitor the airplane’s movement
  • Control ground traffic at airports
  • Provide critical information to pilots while flying
  • Alert staff incase of an emergency

Types of Air Traffic Controllers

  • Must have an Air Traffic Control Tower Operator Certificate
  • At least 18 years old
  • Fluent in English, US Citizen
  • At least 3 years of work experience, or Bachelors degree

Airport Security

Job Discription

  • Search/inspect baggage
  • Screen passengers and staff before boarding
  • Submit incident reports if necessary
  • Authenticate employee identification
  • Evacuate public in case of emergency
  • Work with trained canines to discover drugs and illegal substances

Qualifications

  • Fluent in speaking, reading, and writing in English
  • High school diploma
  • Background in a form of law enforcement is encouraged

Baggage Handler

Job Description

  • Load and unload customer luggage and cargo
  • Organize luggage onto correct flights
  • Operate heavy machinery

Qualifications

  • Able to lift and carry certain amounts of weights
  • Able to work outdoors in extreme weather conditions
  • Over 18 years old
  • Warehouse work experience and LGV license is encouraged

Mechanic

Job Description

  • Performing maintenance on airplane accessories
  • Running tests to ensure the aircraft is safe to fly
  • Maintain repair logs and documents of what has been replaced
  • Keep note of stock supplies and materials they have
  • Installing replacement parts that are damaged

Qualifications

  • Ability to lift heavy items
  • Computer aptitude and experience
  • Must have an FAA approved aviation maintenance technician school qualification
  • At least 18 years of age
  • Certified mechanic

Pilot

Job Discription

  • Performing pre and post flight aircraft inspections
  • Determining risks that may occur and how to fix them
  • Ensuring the safety and comfort of passengers and crew
  • Keeping accurate records for compliance reasons
  • Selecting safe and efficient flight routes

Qualifications

  • Training in military or attending FAA certified flight schools
  • Bachelor's degree is encouraged, but the minimum education requirement is 2 years of college
  • Must have a commercial pilot’s license
  • Transport pilot certificate- at least 23 years old, have 1500 hours of flight time, and pass written and flight exams

Flight Attendant

Job Description

  • Pre-flight safety checks and ensure the plane is clean
  • Offer information and support to passengers for their comfort
  • Show emergency measures (oxygen masks, seatbelts, etc.)
  • Verify tickets and direct to seat
  • Make analytical reports concerning flight issues
  • Serve beverages and food

Qualifications

  • High school diploma required
  • Cabin crew certification and training required
  • Fluent in English and good communication skills, friendly
  • Fluency in multiple languages is an advantage

Aerospace Engineer

Job Discription

  • Direct and coordinate the design and manufacture of aircraft
  • Determine whether project proposals are technically and financially stable
  • Ensure projects meet standards
  • Inspect damaged products to find problems and solutions
  • Develop acceptance criteria for designs, standards, and completion dates

Qualifications

  • Must have a bachelor's degree in aerospace engineering
  • Must have US citizenship for certain clearances
